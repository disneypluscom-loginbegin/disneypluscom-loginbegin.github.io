Your guide to activating and enjoying Disney+ streaming on any device.

What is DisneyPlus.com/Begin?

DisneyPlus.com/Begin is the activation portal for Disney+ that allows you to set up and start streaming Disney+ content on your devices, like TVs, mobile phones, and tablets. If you've recently signed up for Disney+, here's how you can activate and start watching all your favorite movies and shows!

Steps to Activate Disney+ Using DisneyPlus.com/Begin

Follow these simple steps to activate your account:

  1. Open the Disney+ app on your device, or visit DisneyPlus.com/Begin on your browser.
  2. Sign in with your Disney+ account credentials if prompted.
  3. Locate and note the 8-digit activation code displayed on your device.
  4. Enter the code at DisneyPlus.com/Begin and click "Continue" to link your device to your Disney+ account.
